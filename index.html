AI Research Mentorship

Seeding great minds

Vision

To become a lead research lab in Tanzania for data-driven solutions for sustainable development.

Objectives

  1. To build and strengthen local, regional and international research collaborations that are required to solve the identified challenges in sustainable development goals.
  2. To develop tools (methods, software, algorithms, prototypes) to address real-world problems.
  3. To build the capacity of the rising generation of African data scientists to become productive, competitive, and engaging members of the global scientific community

Supported Projects

  1. Development Projects Success Prediction: On addressing the challenges of managing development projects at the governmental level, we are developing machine learning models to predict project success.
  2. Casual Modeling and Fairness on Poultry Diseases Diagnostics: Fairness, robustness, and trustworthiness are essential when Machine Learning models are deployed in high stakes decisions such as disease diagnosis. We aim to build bias detection methods and models through a causal lens on the dataset we have established for poultry diseases diagnostics. We are developing a mobile application to deploy the models to poultry farmers and livestock officers.
  3. Crop Diseases Identification Model: We have developed a dataset of leaf images for crop diseases identification for maize and bananas . We are developing machine learning models to automate the approach for identification of crop diseases. Our research partner in this work is the Tanzania Agricultural Research Institute in Arusha.

Classes

The AIR Mentorship Class meets every Saturday from 10am at the AI lab. These classes cover a syllabus designed by the tutors. The content covered in each class is available on our github repo and is also listed on this website.

About

To contribute to evidence-based decision-making to address sustainable development challenges through data-driven solutions.

Artificial intelligence, far from being science fiction, is a technology with the potential to transform the economy and improve our lives. AIR Mentorship aims to build a community in Kampala around a common interest in Artificial Intelligence. Starting with 15 students in November 2018, we are just starting to build a base on which we shall train many.
